# Maisha Jewellery — AI Image Generation Prompts

All images should be generated at **1200 × 1200 px, square (1:1 aspect ratio)**.
This matches the storefront product card and product detail display format exactly.

## Rings

You are a professional jewellery product photographer. I will provide you with an original photograph of a ring. Your task is to create a refined, studio-quality version of this exact piece.

**Critical rules — do not break these under any circumstances:**

1. **Stone count is fixed.** Count every polki, kundan, diamond, and gemstone visible on the ring — on the top face, along the sides, and on the shank if any. The recreated ring must show the exact same number of stones in the exact same positions. Not one more, not one fewer.
2. **Shape of every gold piece and kundan setting is fixed.** Study the exact outline and silhouette of every gold element — the top face frame, every petal, border, prong, and shank profile. Study the exact shape of every kundan cell and polki setting. Replicate all of these shapes precisely. Do not round off corners, simplify edges, merge adjacent cells, or invent new shapes. If the top face is oval, it must remain oval. If a kundan cell is square, it must remain square.
3. **Color and finish are fixed.** The metal color (gold, rose gold, silver), stone colors, polki texture, and any enamel must exactly match the original. Do not brighten, tint, or alter any material or finish.
4. **Design and structure are fixed.** The overall shape, proportions, top face design, shank style, and spatial arrangement must remain identical to the original. Do not simplify, add embellishments, or change any structural element.

**Output format:** 1200 × 1200 px square image. Clean white or off-white studio background. Position the ring at whatever angle best fills the square frame and shows the piece clearly — top face, shank, and all stone details visible. Soft even lighting with no harsh shadows, sharp focus across the full ring.

Recreate the ring shown in the original image following all rules above.
